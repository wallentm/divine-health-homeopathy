## Design Philosophy

The website should feel:

- Timeless
- Elegant
- Classical
- Calm
- Warm
- Trustworthy
- Historically grounded

Avoid anything that feels:

- Corporate
- Flashy
- Modern-tech
- Salesy
- Overly animated

## Branding Principles

Classical homeopathy is individualized medicine.

The site should emphasize:

- History
- Scholarship
- Compassion
- Observation
- Careful listening

Avoid exaggerated marketing language.

---

## Photography

Whenever possible, use:

- Mindy's original photography
- Historical homeopathic artifacts
- Antique books
- Botanical illustrations
- Historical pharmacy objects

Avoid generic stock photography.
